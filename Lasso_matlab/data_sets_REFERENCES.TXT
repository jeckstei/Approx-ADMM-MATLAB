These datasets are from following sources.

# finance1000.mat
Dataset created by Yucheng Low from unigram predictors, by removing such unigrams that had over 1000 occurences. Reference:
Kogan, S., Levin, D., Routledge, B.R., Sagi, J.S., and Smith, N.A. Predicting risk from financial reports with regression. In Human Language Tech.-NAACL, 2009.

# Mug32_singlepixcam.mat
# Mug128_singlepixcam.mat
# Logo64_singlepixcam.mat
# Ball64_singlepixcam.mat

From the Single-Pixel Camera project.
Duarte, M.F., Davenport, M.A., Takhar, D., Laska, J.N., Sun, T., Kelly, K.F., and Baraniuk, R.G  Single-pixel imaging via compressive sampling. Signal Processing Magazine, IEEE, 25(2), 2008.

# mug05*, peppers_*
These datasets were created by Aapo Kyrola based on images used in the Single-Pixel Camera Project. However, the measurement matrices are not the same used by the project but generated randomly.

# Sparco*
